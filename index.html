How everything started

Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar opened its store on 26 October 2016 in an Sandy Bay, Hobart. By the end of 2021 Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar will have at least 3 stores operating across Tasmania.

Both management and staff are constantly working together to deliver the freshest and top quality Japanese food to our customers. Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar products are sourced locally and delivered daily to ensure its quality. All Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar chefs are required to undergo a minimum of twelve months training to become an independent chef for each outlet. Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar believes in providing the best environment and coaching system to secure the most suitable staff in each position. Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar management also believes that providing opportunities to the younger staff is vital as they are the future of its business.

Serving Japanese food is a labour of love between our chefs and our customers. Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar has bonded by our love of classical sushi, ramen and has subsequently created our sublime menu that is full of flavours including: Maki Roll, Sushi Box, Sashimi & Salad, Inari, Nigiri, Sushi Platters, Tonkotsu ramen, Shio ramen. Enoki Sushi Ramen Bar signature dishes which appeal to the eyes as well as your palate are the Fresh Tasmanian Salmon Roll; Crispy Free Range Chicken Roll and Tuna Salad Roll. Our sushi boxes, tonkotsu ramen and salad boxes are equally popular. Brown rice option is available on request at selected stores for sushi lovers who are looking for a healthful staple with low GI and high fibre option.
